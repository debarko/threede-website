We are a team of Engineers, Technocrats, Consultants and Designers. We work informally with the objective to deliver quality output. Our commitments to the work ensure output with a lasting effect to our customers and in turn to the society.

Design Engineering

We do system design of Material Handling by Pneumatic conveying, Slurry and water pumping, compressed air system, associated Equipment sizing by established formulae. Moreover, where, no such formula exists, we have our developed formula / software, backed up by various commissioned plant data.

Drawing & Detail Engg

We undertake process diagrams, Layout and General Arrangement Drawings, Survey drawings and also equipment and fabrication drawings. Back up estimation, scheduling and other detailing are also carried out.

Engineering Consultancy

We prepare tender specifications, DBRs, Drawings etc., review and technically evaluate bid offers on behalf of Plant owners.

Quality Audit

We carry out third Party inspection for fabricated items, Castings, Mechanical and Electrical items. Also take care of QA documentation activities.
